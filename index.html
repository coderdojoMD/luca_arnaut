This is a game that imagines a fight where the aliens are trying to capture our planet. You are able to fight aliens, and humans(PvP mode). You can also defend the Earth with bots vs. alien bots. There are many types of aliens and weapons, also a lot of other planets, like Arcadia. The base alien(first alien) is Elite, than appears the Grunt. The Elite is based on laser swords, Grunts are tiny and very hard to shot. Grunts are not dealing a big damage, but Elites are dealing! Sword Elites are easy to kill from big range. Caution: Laser swords are doing the to run faster! In the first versions you were able to play as an Elite(in PvP mode). So now you know there are a lot of types of aliens. But they are hardened! Not all but they are! There are other type of invaders. They are named Floods, they don't want planets, they are in need of alies, they are alien zombies!
